# Threat Landscape

## External Threats

### Phishing

Attackers send fraudulent emails to steal credentials.

### Credential Theft

Attackers attempt to gain access to Microsoft 365 accounts.

### Malware

Malicious software delivered through email attachments or websites.

### Business Email Compromise

Attackers impersonate business executives to commit fraud.

---

## Internal Threats

### Human Error

Employees accidentally share confidential information.

### Weak Passwords

Poor password practices increase security risks.

### Excessive Permissions

Users may have unnecessary access to business systems.

### Accidental Data Deletion

Important files may be removed unintentionally.
